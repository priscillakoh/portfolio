priscilla koh





Art has been a constant throughout my life, an imaginary friend that I could always confide in. I am a Korean American artist based in California, currently pursuing a BFA in Pictorial Art and a BA in Liberal Studies Teacher Preparation-Integrated Teacher Educator Program (LSTP-ITEP) at San Jose State University. As someone who finds it difficult to communicate thoughts and expressions through words, I have been able to articulate them through painting and drawing. Recently, I have been focusing on creating art circulating around my experiences as a first generation eldest immigrant daughter along with my childhood. I believe it is important to reflect on our pasts in order to see how our identities evolved overtime.
